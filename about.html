Typewriter Poet

One-of-a-Kind Performance / Poetry-on-Demand

A tried and tested performance typewriter poet catering one-of-a-kind event experiences.
‍
From weddings, holidays, parties, art openings, birthdays and other occasions, memorialize your special event with Abigail Mott's Poetry Service and guarantee a lasting impression for your guests' experience.

Performing with an early 1900's typewriter, Abigail will invent individualized poems from prompts given by guests on a topic of their choosing. Abigail simulates a periodic atmosphere adding an air of elegance and recreating a time lost in time.
‍
Abigail Mott Poetry caters to those looking for a one-of-a-kind event experience.

People all over the world have fallen in love with Abigail’s warmth, charm, and charisma. Having worked as a performance poet for well over a decade, she has mastered her craft of creating an enchanting, entertaining and approachable environment.

A Place of Truth

Documentary Film

A Place of Truth is a feature-length documentary film that follows Abigail’s early travels throughout the United States and explores the process and connection of her performance as a typewriter poet.

“In making this film, I came to appreciate certain intersecting themes in Abi’s life and creative work: intuition, collaboration and inspired improvisation.

As Abi describes it, her aim is to listen to each customer and get underneath what is said. Her poems often express feelings people have but can’t easily articulate for themselves. ”– Barrett Rudich, Director
